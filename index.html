Sustainable is:

  • Use
  • Re-Use
  • Preserve

    • Use

    Take what's being given to us, Solar Panels to generate electricity, rainwater systems to feed showers, geothermal exchanges to heat, natual sunlight to see.

    Stored rainwater can be used for drinking, showering, and watering plants. Local plants are already adapted to the environment and need less resources.

    Re-Use

    Grey-water from sinks and showers can be used to flush toilets and water plants

    Composting system can feed gardens,

    Preserve

    Proper insulation can reduce heating needs, double or triple pane windows keep temperatures stable inside, thermal masses can store heat in the day and release it at night.

    Energy efficient lighting and applainces reduce demand for electricity. Low flow toilets reduce water consumption
